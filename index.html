Silas Bergen

Associate professor of statistics and data science

Winona State University

Introduction

I am pleased to present my narrative in support of my application to Full Professor, in accordance with the requirements of the IFO/MnSCU Master Agreement, Article 22: Professional Development and Evaluation, and Article 25: Tenure, Promotion, and Non-Renewal. It’s hard to believe that Fall 2023 marks the beginning of my tenth year at Winona State! I continue to love working in the Mathematics and Statistics department. Our students continue to invigorate and inspire me. My colleagues continue to be the best in the business – intelligent, collaborative, and innovative, with a deep care for the learning experience of the students we serve. It’s been a wonderful ten years, and every day I am grateful to be a part of the Winona State community. Here’s to the next ten, and the ten beyond that!

My narrative details my activities of the past five years as Associate professor, January 2019 – December 2023. I would weight my efforts in the five criteria during this time span accordingly:

  • Criterion 1: Teaching effectiveness; 40%
  • Criterion 2: Scholarship and creative achievement; 30%
  • Criterion 3: Continuing preparation; 5%
  • Criterion 4: Student growth and development; 10%
  • Criterion 5: Service to the university and community; 15%
